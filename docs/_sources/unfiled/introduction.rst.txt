Introduction
============

Hi, there! Welcome to VG100, *Introduction to Software Engineering*.

This is a special course among all sections. In this course, you will be employed to a company called `SilverFOCS <https://focs.ji.sjtu.edu.cn/silverfocs/>`_.

You and your teammates will develop two (web) video games in this semester and share your game with other teams on the game day.

You will learn a functional programming language called `Elm <https://elm-lang.org/>`_. You will also learn how to use `Git <https://git-scm.com/>`_ and `Gitea <https://focs.ji.sjtu.edu.cn/git/>`_ to manage your code.
